About Us

Welcome to The City Gym, where fitness meets passion. At The City Gym, we believe in empowering individuals to lead healthier, happier lives through personalized fitness solutions, expert guidance, and a supportive community environment.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where people from all walks of life can come together to pursue their fitness goals, regardless of their age, background, or fitness level. What started as a small fitness center has now evolved into a thriving community hub dedicated to transforming lives through the power of fitness.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence in everything we do. From our state-of-the-art facilities to our team of experienced trainers and instructors, we strive to provide our members with the tools, support, and inspiration they need to achieve their fitness aspirations.

But more than just a gym, The City Gym is a place where friendships are forged, goals are conquered, and dreams are realized. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, you'll find a home here at The City Gym.

Join us and experience the difference at The City Gym. Together, we'll redefine what it means to be fit, healthy, and unstoppable. We provide many facilities, including:

  • Personal training
  • Bodybuilding and men's physique competition preparation
  • Diabetic and cholesterol control programs

Our mission is to promote health and wellness.

Membership Plans

Basic Plan:

Our Basic Plan offers fundamental access to our gym facilities. With this plan, you'll have the opportunity to utilize state-of-the-art equipment and workout spaces designed to help you reach your fitness objectives. You're a beginner or a seasoned gym-goer, this plan provides a solid foundation for your workouts.

Offerings:

  • Access to gym facilities: Utilize our range of cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, and functional training areas to customize your workout routines.
  • Professional guidance: Benefit from basic support and guidance from our fitness staff to help you get started on your fitness journey.

Price: ₹800/month
Premium Plan:

Upgrade your fitness experience with our Premium Plan, which includes all the benefits of the Basic Plan plus access to a variety of classes. Whether you're interested in group fitness, yoga, or specialized training sessions, this plan offers a diverse range of classes to suit your preferences and fitness goals.

Offerings:

  • Access to gym facilities: Enjoy all the amenities available with the Basic Plan, including access to our cutting-edge equipment and facilities.
  • Classes: Participate in a selection of fitness classes led by experienced instructors, designed to challenge and inspire you while providing a fun and supportive environment.

Price: ₹1000/month
VIP Plan:

For the ultimate fitness experience, our VIP Plan offers all-inclusive access to our gym and its premium offerings. With this plan, you'll have access to everything our facility has to offer, ensuring that you have the resources and support you need to maximize your fitness potential.

Offerings:

  • Access to gym facilities: Experience unrestricted access to our gym facilities, including all equipment, workout areas, and amenities.
  • Classes: Enjoy unlimited access to all fitness classes offered at our gym, allowing you to diversify your workout routine and explore new fitness modalities.
  • Personalized support: Receive personalized attention and support from our expert trainers, who will work with you to develop customized workout plans and provide ongoing guidance and motivation.

Price: ₹1500/month

Services

At The City Gym, we offer a comprehensive range of fitness services designed to meet the diverse needs and goals of our members. Our goal is to provide you with everything you need to succeed on your fitness journey, all under one roof.

  1. Personal Training: Our team of certified personal trainers is here to provide you with personalized guidance, support, and motivation every step of the way. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, improve your athletic performance, or just lead a healthier lifestyle, our trainers will create a customized workout plan tailored to your unique goals and abilities.
  2. Group Fitness Classes: From high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga, Pilates, cycling, and more, our group fitness classes offer something for everyone. Led by experienced instructors in a fun and energizing atmosphere, our classes are designed to challenge your body and inspire your spirit, regardless of your fitness level.
  3. Nutritional Counseling: We believe that nutrition is the foundation of a healthy lifestyle. That's why we offer personalized nutritional counseling services to help you make informed choices about your diet and achieve optimal results. Whether you're looking to lose weight, gain muscle, or simply improve your overall health, our nutrition experts will work with you to develop a sustainable eating plan that fits your lifestyle and preferences.
  4. Functional Training: Our functional training area is equipped with a variety of functional fitness equipment, including kettlebells, battle ropes, TRX suspension trainers, and more. Whether you're rehabilitating from an injury, improving your athletic performance, or just looking to move better and feel stronger in your everyday life, our functional training programs will help you build functional strength, mobility, and stability.
  5. Specialized Programs: From boot camps and weight loss challenges to sports-specific training and corporate wellness programs, we offer a variety of specialized fitness programs to meet the unique needs and interests of our members. Whatever your goals may be, we'll help you find the perfect program to achieve them.

At The City Gym, we're committed to helping you unlock your full potential and live your best life. Come see what sets us apart and join the The City Gym family today!
